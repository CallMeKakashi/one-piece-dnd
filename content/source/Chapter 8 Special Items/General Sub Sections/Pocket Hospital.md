### Pocket Hospital

A small, handheld device in the shape of a heart with a plus sign decorating it, that is loaded with the ability to manufacture chemicals. When wielder by a Medic, it allows them to use their knowledge to form the chemicals into fast acting medicine.
The device has 10 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to use one of the following creations from it, using your creativity save DC and creative ability modifier: Cure Wounds (1 charge per creation level, up to 4th), Lesser Restoration (2 charges), or Mass Cure Wounds (5 charges).
The device regains all expended charges daily at dawn.
