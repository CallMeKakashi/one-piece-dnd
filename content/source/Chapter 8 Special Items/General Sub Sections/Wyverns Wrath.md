### Wyvern's Wrath

A Supreme Grade Greatsword with a scale-like pattern across its blade, as well as a green handle with a claw motif. This is a +3 Greatsword, it deals 2d8 slashing instead of 2d6, and is cursed. At the start of each of the wielder's turns when using this weapon in combat, they take 1d8 psychic damage. Once they overcome the curse, the weapon deals an extra 1d8 psychic damage instead.
