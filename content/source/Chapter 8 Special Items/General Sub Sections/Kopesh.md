### Kopesh

A Supreme Grade scimitar that has become a black blade. It shines as if brand new, yet black as night. The handle and guard are golden and decorated with eye-like patterns. The is a +3 Scimitar, except it also has a +2 bonus to damage rolls, is effectively unbreakable, and deals 2d6 slashing instead of 1d6 slashing.
