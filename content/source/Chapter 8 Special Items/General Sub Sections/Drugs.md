### Drugs

Drugs are a dark, but ultimately useful side of science and medicine. They can bring those beyond their normal strength, but it is best to keep them in moderation.
##### Energy Steroids
Energy Steroids are a special drug that multiplies the consumer's strength, doubling it. However, it also destroys your youth after the effects.
- Taking an energy steroid will increase all your physical ability scores by 2 for the next 10 minutes.
Taking multiple resets the duration and increases yet another 2 times, to a maximum of 30.
- Once the duration wears off, the user gains 5 levels of exhaustion and has their natural life reduced by 2^(the number of Energy Steroids taken) years.
