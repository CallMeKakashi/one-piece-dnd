### Hermit

You lived in seclusion – either in a sheltered community such as a monastery, or entirely alone – for a formative part of your life. In your time apart from the clamor of society, you found quiet, solitude, and perhaps some of the answers you were looking for.
Skill Proficiencies: 2 from Medicine, Religion, Nature, Survival
Tool Proficiencies: Herbalism kit
Equipment: 100,000 Berries; Small Medkits x2; Rations x5, Herbalism kit, A notebook full of notes.
#### Feature: Secluded Study
While living in seclusion, you have practiced extensively a specific set of skills unparalleled. You gain your choice of the [Skill Expert Feat (Pg. 224)](#p224) or the [Sound Intelligence Feat (Pg. 224)](#p224).
#### Suggested Characteristics
Some hermits are well suited to a life of seclusion, whereas others chafe against it and long for company. Whether they embrace solitude or long to escape it, solitary life shapes their attitudes and ideals. A few are driven slightly mad by their years apart from society.
