### Medical Specialization

At 2nd level, you choose a medical specialization for your focused studies. Choose between the field surgeon, physician, chemist, amputator, or plaguewright, all detailed at the end of the class description.
Your choice grants you features at 2nd level, and again at 6th, 10th, and 14th level.
