### Roguish Archetype

At 3rd Level, you choose an archetype that you emulate in the exercise of your rogue abilities: Seeker, Thief, Assassin, Inquisitive, Gadget Trickster, Fencer, Scout, Swashbuckler, Sawbones, Ringmaster, Wildcard, and Bruiser are detailed at the end of the class description.
