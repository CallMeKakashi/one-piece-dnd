### Bard College

At 3rd Level, you delve into the advanced techniques of a bard college of your choice: the College of Legends, the College of Lore, the College of Eloquence, the College of The Idol, the College of Swords, and the College of Bewitchment are detailed at the end of the class description. Your choice grants you features at 3rd Level and again at 6th and 14th level.
