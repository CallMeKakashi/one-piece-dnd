### Extended Release

At 7th level, your Experimental Medicine has long lasting effects, even in combat.
When you use your Experimental Medicine feature, and you lose your temporary hit points, you still retain the beneifts for a number of hours equal to your Wisdom modifier, or until your hit points reach 0.
