### Marksman Archetype

At 3rd level, you choose an archetype to emulate: Sniper, Bounty Hunter, Beastmaster, Nighthawk, Swarmkeeper, Olympian, or Guru. All are detailed at the end of the class description. Your choice grants you features at 3rd Level and again at 7th, 11th, and 15th level.
