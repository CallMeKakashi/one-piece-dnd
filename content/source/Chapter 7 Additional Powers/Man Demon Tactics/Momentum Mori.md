### Momentum Mori

At 20th level, you have mastered the art of Man Demon Tactics, turning all of your momentum into a devastating force. Your critical hit range increases by 1 for melee weapons, and your melee weapons attacks ignore resistances to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
