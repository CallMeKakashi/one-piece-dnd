### Foxfire Points

When you gain this ability, you gain a number of Foxfire points equal to your proficiency bonus. You can expend these Foxfire points to fuel your Foxfire features. You regain all expended Foxfire points at the end a long rest.
When you reach 5th level, your expended Foxfire points are regained at the end of a short or long rest.
