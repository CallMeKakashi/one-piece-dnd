## Fortune Telling

*Prerequisites: A Charisma, Intelligence, or Wisdom score of 16 or higher*
You have spent decades honing your craft, the ability to divine the the fortunes of yourself and others, be it via a crystal ball, or even performing augury, you have a knack for knowing what comes ahead.
