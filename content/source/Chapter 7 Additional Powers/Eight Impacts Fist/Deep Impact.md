### Deep Impact

At 15th level, the range of your shockwaves. Once per turn, when you use your First Impacts feature, you can give an unarmed strike a damage spread of 5ft. The damage spread DC equals 8 + your Strength or Dexterity modifier + you proficiency bonus. You are unaffected by this damage spread.
